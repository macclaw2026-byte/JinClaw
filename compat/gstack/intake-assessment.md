# Jin-gstack Intake Assessment

## Objective
Absorb the strongest software-factory mechanisms from Jin-gstack into JinClaw while preventing control-plane replacement, governance drift, unsafe hidden coupling, or duplicate skill confusion.

## Accepted Core Principle
Retain the full lifecycle:
- think
- plan
- build
- review
- test
- ship
- reflect

JinClaw may adapt wording and routing, but must not amputate this lifecycle.

## Accept
- planning discipline
- review/test/ship closure discipline
- skill generation ideas
- browser/QA methodology patterns
- evaluation and evidence culture

## Reject
- replacing JinClaw control center
- replacing JinClaw governance or constitution
- raw skill namespace flooding
- upstream voice/persona takeover
- direct unsafe coupling to critical runtime paths

## Intake Modes
- borrow concept
- adapt via compat layer
- reimplement natively
- sandbox and prove before promotion
- reject
