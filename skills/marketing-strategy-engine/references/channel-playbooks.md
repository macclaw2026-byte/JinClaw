# Channel Playbooks

## Email

Use when:

- business email is plausible
- account context is strong
- risk budget is available

Need:

- low repetition
- clear identity
- clear unsubscribe path

## Form

Use when:

- website is real
- email quality is uncertain
- brand risk is high

Need:

- concise first ask
- no repetitive submissions

## Social

Use when:

- role is identifiable
- social profile is active
- relationship can begin softly

Need:

- low-friction opening
- context-aware tone

## Partner / Dealer Application

Use when:

- the account is a channel candidate
- the site exposes a formal application path

Need:

- partnership-fit framing
- business legitimacy proof
