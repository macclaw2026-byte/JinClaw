# Outreach Execution And Risk

## Automation boundaries

### Manual

Use for:

- high-value accounts
- legal/pricing-sensitive messaging
- high-ambiguity partner outreach

### Semi-automated

Use for:

- reviewed tasks
- controlled send queues
- human approval before release

### Controlled automated

Use for:

- low-risk channels
- validated targets
- low-volume paced execution

## Risk gates

Check before execution:

- suppression status
- invalid history
- complaint risk
- repetition limit
- channel cooldown
- approval state

## Stop conditions

- unsubscribe
- do_not_contact
- hard bounce / invalid recipient
- complaint-risk escalation
- repeated failed form/social route
