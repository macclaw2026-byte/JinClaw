# Customer Voice Routing Examples

## Example: Toe spacers

### Feed to product-selection-engine
- recurring_pain_points: 脚痛 / PF / bunion / toe splay
- expected_benefits: 缓痛 / 舒适 / 足部功能改善
- objections: 全脚趾款不舒服，款式适配差
- implication: 子品类拆分空间明显

### Feed to us-market-growth-engine
- buyer language: “Do they actually do anything?”, “worth it?”, “got rid of my pain”
- messaging implication: 不要空喊矫正，强调具体使用场景、舒适度、体感改善

## Example: Drawer organizer

### Feed to product-selection-engine
- recurring_pain_points: 尺寸不合 / 浪费空间 / 不够灵活
- expected_benefits: 可调节 / 可堆叠 / 低成本 / 多场景
- implication: 更细分的 modular/cross-use organizer 有价值

### Feed to us-market-growth-engine
- buyer language: “fit correctly”, “super easy”, “super inexpensive”, “stack”
- messaging implication: 强调 fit、modular、easy、affordable，而不是抽象整理
