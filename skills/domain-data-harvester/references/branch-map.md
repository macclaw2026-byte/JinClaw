# Branch Map

## Product-selection branch
Use for:
- product ideas
- trend validation
- marketplace proxies
- competitor depth
- manufacturability clues
- blue-ocean inspiration

Good source families:
- trend pages
- marketplace pages
- search/discovery surfaces
- Pinterest / visual inspiration surfaces
- Etsy / handmade-idea and niche-demand surfaces
- supplier/manufacturer clues
- crowdfunding/new-product platforms

Best downstream skills:
- product-selection-engine
- resilient-external-research
- safe-learning-log
- runtime-evolution-loop

## Marketing-intelligence branch
Use for:
- positioning
- offer analysis
- customer pain points
- messaging angles
- competitor landing pages
- content/channel research

Good source families:
- brand websites
- landing pages
- ad/SEO surfaces
- review pages
- communities/forums
- comparison pages

Best downstream skills:
- us-market-growth-engine
- resilient-external-research
- safe-learning-log

## Customer-voice branch
Use for:
- review mining
- complaints
- unmet needs
- recurring language patterns
- fuzzy demand / wish-list style requests
- tool-gap statements ("I need X but can't find a good tool")

Good source families:
- reviews
- forums
- Reddit
- community Q&A
- comparison articles

Best downstream skills:
- product-selection-engine
- us-market-growth-engine

## Crowdfunding / blue-ocean branch
Use for:
- new product concepts
- early demand signals
- feature innovation patterns
- problem/solution framing from novel products

Good source families:
- Kickstarter
- Indiegogo
- product-launch sites
- innovation showcases

Best downstream skills:
- product-selection-engine
- runtime-evolution-loop
- safe-learning-log
