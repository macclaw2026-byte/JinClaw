# Prospect Data Engine Source Strategy

## Source tiers

### Tier 1: Primary account anchors

- official website
- contact/team page
- about page
- case studies / portfolio
- Google Business Profile
- LinkedIn company page

Use these to confirm:

- business exists
- domain is real
- industry / offer / geography are plausible

### Tier 2: Market directories and partner surfaces

- trade directories
- association member directories
- exhibitor lists
- partner/dealer directories
- chamber / regional business listings

Use these to expand discovery, but validate against Tier 1 before promoting into the main database.

### Tier 3: Context and signal surfaces

- blog/news pages
- hiring pages
- social pages
- review pages
- press mentions

Use these to derive:

- freshness
- growth signals
- buying context
- partner/channel fit

## Search recipe model

Each recipe should specify:

- objective
- query family
- source family
- extraction target
- expected fields
- validation rules
- rejection rules

## Evidence rules

Keep for each surviving field:

- source_url
- source_type
- captured_at
- extraction_method
- confidence

Never drop provenance.
