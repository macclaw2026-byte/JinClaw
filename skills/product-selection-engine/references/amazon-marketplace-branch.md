# Amazon Marketplace Branch

Use this branch when the user specifically wants Amazon product selection.

Use the shared monitoring standard from `../../self-cognition-orchestrator/references/monitoring-and-retrofit-framework.md`.

## Core strategy

Do not chase the flashiest trend. Prefer products that can realistically produce steady daily order flow with survivable competition.

Target shape:
- stable demand
- moderate competition, not zero competition
- no heavy compliance burden
- no food / ingestible / body-contact formulation / health-regulated category
- no strong brand dependence
- no obvious IP or patent-copying posture
- simple listing, shipping, and returns profile

## What “good” looks like

A strong Amazon candidate often looks like:
- useful and boring rather than glamorous
- understandable in one sentence
- light or operationally simple
- not dominated by giant brands
- capable of modest but steady daily volume
- improvable via bundle, packaging, accessory set, small functional improvement, or clearer positioning

## Demand target lens

Do not require huge category volume.

Often prefer niches that look capable of:
- roughly tens to low hundreds of orders per day at the niche/product-cluster level
- steady movement over time rather than short-lived breakout hype

## Competition filter

Reject or downgrade when:
- the listing field looks like same-product commodity warfare
- review moats look overwhelming for a new entrant
- price compression is severe
- the category appears to require strong brand trust to convert

## Recommended research questions

- does this category look stable rather than hype-driven?
- could a smaller seller plausibly enter without a giant review moat?
- can the offer be improved in a practical way?
- is the product low enough risk from compliance, safety, and returns?
- can the product sell steadily without relying on a famous brand?

## Good candidate patterns

Often stronger:
- practical accessories
- household utility products
- organization / storage helpers
- non-regulated personal-use tools or accessories
- simple family / baby utility items that are not formulation-heavy or medically sensitive

Often weaker:
- supplements
- food and drinks
- shampoo / body wash / formulation-heavy body-contact categories
- medical / quasi-medical products
- heavily branded fashion mimicry
- patent-sensitive gadget clones


## Competitor-link requirement

When returning shortlisted products for Amazon, try to include a real public Amazon competitor/product page link for each candidate so the user can inspect what the live product actually looks like.

Preferred order:
- direct product page
- stable search results page for the exact product phrase
- category/subcategory page if direct product pages are not reliably available

Do not fabricate exact product URLs. If certainty is low, label the link as approximate or use a search/category surface instead.
