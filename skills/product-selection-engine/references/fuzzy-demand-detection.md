# Fuzzy Demand Detection

Use this reference when users are not naming a clean product directly, but are still revealing real unmet demand.

## What to look for

Signals such as:
- “I need something that can…”
- “Where can I find…”
- “I wish there was a tool/product that…”
- “Nothing I tried really works”
- DIY hacks that suggest the market is compensating for a missing product

## Why this matters

Some opportunities appear before the market converges on a standardized keyword or product format.

## Good source surfaces
- Reddit
- forums
- Pinterest
- Etsy
- Q&A pages
- niche communities
- DIY / maker discussions

## Output fields
- hidden demand statement
- current workaround
- likely productized solution
- adjacent existing products
- marketplace translation potential
- manufacturability clue
- confidence
