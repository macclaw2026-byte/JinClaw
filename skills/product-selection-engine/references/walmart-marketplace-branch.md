# Walmart Marketplace Branch

Use this branch when the user specifically wants Walmart product selection.

Use the shared monitoring standard from `../../self-cognition-orchestrator/references/monitoring-and-retrofit-framework.md`.

## Core strategy

For Walmart, prefer practical broad-market products with steady demand, low friction, and low brand dependence.

Target shape:
- everyday usefulness
- simple value proposition
- stable sales rhythm
- low compliance burden
- non-food / non-ingestible / non-body-contact-regulated categories
- limited brand dependence
- manageable operational complexity

## What “good” looks like

A strong Walmart candidate often looks like:
- mainstream and easy to understand
- value-conscious but not race-to-the-bottom junk
- useful in home, family, auto, storage, organization, or simple utility contexts
- able to move consistently without requiring premium brand aura

## Demand target lens

Prefer products that suggest:
- steady practical demand
- repeatable sales behavior
- enough volume to matter, but not such intense attention that competition becomes irrational

## Competition filter

Downgrade when:
- the category is dominated by giant household brands with little room for smaller sellers
- the category depends on strong formulation trust or safety-sensitive claims
- the category is too commoditized to maintain reasonable economics

## Recommended research questions

- does the product solve an ordinary everyday problem clearly?
- can it win on usefulness and value without famous branding?
- is the category operationally simple enough for steady marketplace execution?
- are returns, breakage, and complaints likely to stay manageable?
- does it avoid unnecessary regulatory or certification burden?

## Good candidate patterns

Often stronger:
- household utility
- organization and storage
- practical family products
- simple auto accessories
- low-risk convenience products

Often weaker:
- food and drinks
- supplements and medicines
- shampoo / body wash / chemistry-heavy body-contact products
- highly aspirational branded fashion plays
- categories where trust is mostly driven by major brands


## Competitor-link requirement

When returning shortlisted products for Walmart, try to include a real public Walmart competitor/product page link for each candidate so the user can inspect what the live product actually looks like.

Preferred order:
- direct product page
- stable search results page for the exact product phrase
- category/subcategory page if direct product pages are not reliably available

Do not fabricate exact product URLs. If certainty is low, label the link as approximate or use a search/category surface instead.
